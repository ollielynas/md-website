## Racing Simulation

3D racing simulator.

To turn left run this function:
$$L_{eftTurn}=\ w_{heel}\ \to\ \max\left(w_{heel}\ -\ 0.7,-\frac{\pi}{6}\right)$$

To turn right 
$$R_{ightTurn}=w_{heel}\to\min\left(w_{heel}\ +\ 0.7,\frac{\pi}{6}\right)$$
